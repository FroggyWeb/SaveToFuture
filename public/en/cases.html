Handover logins and passwords

Purpose

Transferring over to a relatives or trusted persons the logins and passwrods to your accounts, which you wish to share with him/them in case of your death, missing, disability, or other reasons.

Why SaveToFuture?

You cannot disclosure your passwords to anyone, include law firms and other people. You also should not give passwords to any of your relatives ahead of time. Failing to do so, you are under critical risk of loosing control for your accounts and/or looking contant of these accounts.
